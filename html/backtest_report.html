CANSLIM

The CANSLIM strategy selects stocks that meet certain growth, volume, and momentum criteria:

  • C (Current Earnings): Strong quarterly EPS growth (≥25%)
  • A (Annual Earnings): Robust annual EPS growth (≥20%)
  • N (New Highs): Stocks reaching new 52-week highs
  • S (Supply/Demand): Volume spikes indicating heightened investor interest
  • L (Leader): Stocks outperforming the broader market
  • I (Institutional Sponsorship): Evidence of 'up' days with volume surges
  • M (Market Trend): Invested in equities only when the overall market is in an uptrend

If multiple stocks qualify under these conditions, the top 6 are chosen based on a scoring system that considers recent returns and other momentum factors, ensuring the final selection focuses on the most promising candidates. If no suitable stocks meet these criteria, the strategy defaults to a money market proxy. Rebalancing is performed quarterly.
