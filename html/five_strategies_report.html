5-Strategy Comparison

Market Only Strategy: Always invests 100% in the market proxy (e.g., SPY). A passive approach with no timing or selection element.

Risk-Managed Market Strategy: Invests fully in the market proxy only if a 50-day over 200-day moving average is bullish; otherwise moves to a money market proxy.

Flattened SPY: Equal-weights all current S&P 500 members, rather than using market-cap weighting.

LeaderSPY: Weights all S&P 500 constituents by a 'L_measure' reflecting recent outperformance, giving higher allocations to stronger momentum or 'leadership' stocks.

VolumeSPY: Weights S&P 500 constituents by their last quarter's volume, tilting toward higher-liquidity names.
